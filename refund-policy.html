replay 1. Cancellation & Refund Schedule

Your refund amount depends on when you cancel relative to your scheduled delivery date and time:

  • 48+ hours before delivery: Full refund (100%) — no questions asked
  • 24–48 hours before delivery: 50% refund of the total amount paid
  • Less than 24 hours before delivery: No refund. Preparation has begun and resources have been committed.
  • After delivery: No refund except in cases of service failure (see below)

event_repeat 2. Rescheduling Policy

We encourage rescheduling over cancellation where possible:

  • 48+ hours before delivery: Free rescheduling to any available date
  • 24–48 hours before delivery: ₦5,000 rescheduling fee applies
  • Less than 24 hours: Rescheduling subject to availability; standard rescheduling fee applies
  • Rescheduling can only be done once per booking at the discounted rate

error 3. Service Failure Refunds

We stand behind our service. If we fail to deliver as promised, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund in the following cases:

  • Non-delivery with no prior notice or reasonable explanation
  • Significant deviation from the booked package (e.g., wrong items, major quality issues)
  • Delivery significantly outside the agreed time window without communication

Service failure claims must be reported within 24 hours of the scheduled delivery with supporting evidence (photos, descriptions). We will investigate and respond within 48 hours.

account_balance 4. Refund Processing

Once a refund is approved, processing times depend on your payment method:

  • Bank Transfer: 3–5 business days
  • Credit/Debit Card: 5–10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • Mobile Money (Opay, PalmPay): 1–2 business days
  • USSD Payment: 3–5 business days

Refunds are returned to the original payment method only. We cannot process refunds to a different account.

block 5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable in all circumstances:

  • Customized or personalized items that have been prepared
  • Perishable items (flowers, food, cakes) once prepared
  • Rescheduling fees
  • Delivery fees for completed deliveries

how_to_reg 6. How to Request a Refund

To request a cancellation or refund:

  • Log into your account and go to My Bookings
  • Find the booking and click "Cancel Booking"
  • Or contact us directly: +234 801 234 5678 or hello@wowsurprises.com

Please have your booking reference number ready when contacting us.
