Refund Policy

Last updated: December 15, 2024

1. Overview

At WOW Surprises, we want you to be completely satisfied with our services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancellations and refunds.

We understand that plans can change, and we've designed our policy to be fair to both our customers and our business operations.

2. Cancellation Timeframes

✅ 48+ Hours Before Event

Full Refund (100%)

Cancel 48 hours or more before your scheduled event and receive a complete refund to your original payment method.

⚠️ 24-48 Hours Before Event

Partial Refund (50%)

Cancel between 24-48 hours before your event and receive 50% refund. The remaining 50% covers preparation costs already incurred.

❌ Less Than 24 Hours

No Refund (0%)

Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the event are non-refundable as vendors and staff have already been committed.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Log into your account at WOW Surprises
  2. Go to "My Bookings" and find your booking
  3. Click "Cancel Booking" and follow the prompts
  4. Or contact us directly:
    • Email: refunds@wowsurprises.com
    • Phone: +234 801 234 5678
    • WhatsApp: +234 801 234 5678

4. Refund Processing Time

Once your refund is approved:

  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days (depends on your bank)
  • Mobile Money: 1-2 business days

You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed.

5. Rescheduling vs. Cancelling

Prefer to reschedule instead? We offer flexible rescheduling options:

  • 48+ hours before: Reschedule for free
  • 24-48 hours before: Small rescheduling fee (₦5,000)
  • Less than 24 hours: Subject to availability and fees

Rescheduling allows you to keep your full payment toward a future date!

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable regardless of cancellation timing:

  • Custom-made items (personalized cakes, custom decorations, etc.)
  • Perishable goods already prepared or purchased
  • Third-party vendor fees already paid
  • Special orders or imports
  • Services already rendered

These items will be clearly marked during the booking process.

7. Service Failure or Issues

What if something goes wrong?

If we fail to deliver services as promised, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund:

  • Complete service failure: Full refund
  • Late delivery (our fault): Partial refund or credit
  • Missing items: Refund for missing items
  • Quality issues: Case-by-case evaluation

Please report issues within 24 hours of your event for fastest resolution.

8. Force Majeure

In cases of circumstances beyond our control (severe weather, natural disasters, government restrictions, etc.):

  • We will work with you to reschedule at no additional cost
  • If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund will be provided
  • Costs already incurred may be deducted (materials, deposits)

9. Disputed Refunds

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Provide supporting documentation
  3. We will review your case within 5 business days
  4. If unresolved, escalate to management
