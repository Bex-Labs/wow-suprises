Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

verified_user

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

info 1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, make a booking, or contact us for support:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address
  • Payment Information: Payment is processed securely by Flutterwave. We do not store your card details.
  • Booking Information: Details about the surprises you book, recipient information, and preferences
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website, pages visited, and features used

settings 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your surprise bookings
  • Send booking confirmations, updates, and delivery notifications
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our services and personalize your experience
  • Send promotional communications (you can opt out at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations

share 3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Delivery partners, payment processors (Flutterwave), and SMS/email service providers who help us deliver our services
  • Merchants: Relevant booking details shared with the merchant fulfilling your order
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

lock 4. Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our website
  • Secure authentication via Supabase with industry-standard protocols
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

cookie 5. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect some site functionality.

person 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

child_care 7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

update 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
